RMB = Right Mouse Button

Telekinesis Controls:
Basic Use                      - Hold RMB on most objects, enemies.
Kill Entity in Hand            - While holding RMB and an enemy press the F key or Kick key.
Eat through terrain            - While holding RMB press Down or the S key.

Hentai, I Am Tank, and Thunder Striker Controls:
Basic Use                      - Hold RMB to fire.
